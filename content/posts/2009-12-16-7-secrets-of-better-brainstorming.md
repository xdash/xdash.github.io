最近在上的策划 / 公关 / 广告效果评估等课程均以班级小组形式推进课程作业的进行。在此期间时常需要跟本小组组员进行协调磋商，也就是传说中的头脑风暴。虽说只是催生创意的辅助工具，不过头脑风暴也是一门学问。我和组员就曾多次有意无意地犯下一些脑暴过程中的禁忌。

之前曾经总结过，<a href="http://www.fanbing.net/better-thinking-pattern-5-tips.html" target="_blank">有意识地意识到自己的意识是一种意识</a>。于是我从国外网站找了一些关于头脑风暴原则方法的文章，并以原文形式转载一篇，简单进行总结性意译，以指导今后的脑暴行为，使之妥善高效地走上正轨。

**7 Secrets of Better Brainstorming** (<a href="http://www.masternewmedia.org/news/2008/03/14/effective_brainstorming_7_tips_to.htm" target="_blank">via</a>)

_by Ken Thompson_

**1. Sharpen the Focus 明确焦点**

 ****

&#8220;Start with a well-honed statement of the problem.&#8221;

The more focus you have at the start of a brainstorming session, the more likely that the participants will contribute useful and relevant ideas to it. Make a special effort in clarifying beyond doubt what the mission is and what needs to be found.

// 明确目标，聚焦主题。

// 个人观点补充：需要在头脑风暴正式开场之前，让所有参加者提前明确讨论主题，以形成初步构想。临阵磨枪，现暴现想，往往效率低下。

<!--more-->

**2. Playful Rules 不计较法则**

 ****

&#8220;Don&#8217;t start to critique or debate and encourage wild ideas.&#8221;

**Never criticize or put down someone else idea just because it doesn&#8217;t fit your expectations. Use other people contributions to spin off new ideas and do not waste any time criticizing or explaining why someone&#8217;s else idea is not good. Just keep contributing new stuff with a positive, constructive attitude.**

// 不要否定他人观点，而是在他人观点基础上，生发出你自己的具有创造性的想法。

// 个人观点补充：我认为最为重要的原则。遗憾的是很多时候往往被忽视。头脑风暴是一场相互启发的聚会，而不是压制异议的辩论。或许他人立场错误，但方法或许给你提示。又或者他人完全不靠谱，但脱口而出的某个词成为你灵光一现的爆点。最忌讳的是否定他人的观点却无法提出具有建设性的意见。这除了打击团队的积极性外，没有任何作用。

**3. Number Your Ideas 汇集大量想法**

 ****

&#8220;Go for quantity &#8211; 100 ideas per hours is good!&#8221;

The more ideas you can collect in a brainstorming session the better. Spend time after the session to identify, single out and number the most valuable ones. Better: during the brainstorming session as new ideas come up number and note them down on a public board visible by everyone so that other can keep using and referencing them in the process.

// 尽可能多地提出创意，并与其他人分享，以便互相启发。

// 个人观点补充：数量<>质量。但大量的想法中总有值得继续深入发散挖掘的点。所以尽可能大胆地提出自己的各种不切实际的想法吧。但请遵守第二法则。

**4. Build and Jump 敲定并推进**

 ****

&#8220;When the energy starts to fade, a facilitator should build on an idea or take a jump.&#8221;

If at any one point during a brainstorming session you feel the energy and the contributions are slowing down, take the one best idea that has emerged so far and take it up for dissection, analysis and as a spin-off for more ideas.

// 当整个头脑风暴创意逐渐枯竭时，是时候选择最好的点子进行分析验证并以此发散更多点子了。

// 个人观点补充：如果仍缺乏好点子&#8230;大家洗洗睡吧，好点子总在不经意间悄然而至。前提是不会延误工期。

**5. The Space Remembers 借助形象化工具**

**<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2220" title="brainstorming-capture-your-ideas" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/brainstorming-capture-your-ideas_id2315641_size220.jpg" alt="brainstorming-capture-your-ideas" width="220" height="147" />**

&#8220;Capture the ideas in a medium visible to the whole group.&#8221;

Mind-mapping and visual storyboarding of your brainstorming outputs can be tremendously effective. Especially when you can use the talent of one of your brainstormers to convert simple concepts and ideas into immediately recognizable icons and images.

// 使用思维导图、故事板等工具，将现有创意概念转化为可视的标志、图片等。

// 个人观点补充：思维导图中，以图形代替文字，往往更能碰撞出灵感的火花。当人们启动形象化思维时，负责创意灵感的右脑便被激活。

**6. Stretch Your Mental Muscles 头脑风暴前的热身**

**<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-2221" title="brainstorming-crossword-puzzle" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2009/12/brainstorming-crossword-puzzle_id5286261_size200.jpg" alt="brainstorming-crossword-puzzle" width="200" height="150" />**

&#8220;Warm up if the group are new to each other or don&#8217;t regularly brainstorm or likely to be distracted by other pressing matters.&#8221;

Breaking the ice and warming up the conversational spirit among brainstormers is of the essence to obtain good results. Playing some intellectual game or running a joke competition before brainstorming can often give the right &#8220;zing&#8221; to the team that needs to focus later on new ideas.

// 头脑风暴前最好创建一个活跃、轻松、自在的氛围，例如益智游戏、讲笑话等。

// 个人观点补充：具体方式还是看小组成员的构成。例如跟女生聊八卦是个不错的开场&#8230;

**7. Get Physical 动起来！**

 ****

&#8220;Encourage diagrams, stick figures (2-D), mock-ups and models (3-D).&#8221;

Visualizing new ideas is very effective. The more you can involve your body and the rest of the team in doing so as well, the more peculiar and effective the results that can stem from this process.

// 使想法变得可视化非常有效。让大家动起来。

// 个人观点补充：动动更健康，顺便暖身子。

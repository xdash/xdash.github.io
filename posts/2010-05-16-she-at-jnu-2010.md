听歌大抵是需要心境的，追星也只是特定年代的记忆。曾经陪伴年轻时的自己走过无数日日夜夜的青春激情，多年之后是否芳菲已尽，不得而知。但事隔经年能在异乡求学时再次听到熟悉的三个声音在耳畔响起，感动依旧。

<!--more-->

  
[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3686" title="IMG_0010" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/IMG_0010.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][2]

昨晚在学校文浩馆观看了SHE无锡歌迷见面会。之前曾经在江苏省十六届省运会上看到过活生生的“射”。这次比上次距离更近一步。场馆内爆满，场馆外的壮观景象也让人感叹三人的巨大影响力。原来在我们学校里一直潜伏着众多射友。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3689" title="IMG_0037" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/IMG_0037.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][3]

同来的还有SHE的师弟周定纬，暖场嘉宾+合唱演出。我对此热并不熟悉，只知道此人是台湾选秀节目诞生的新人。场馆内甚至有周定纬后援会的粉丝派发宣传卡，你们真专业。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3690" title="game" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/game.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][4]

暖场游戏，“左一拳，右一拳，大家一起缩一拳”。之前在《娱乐百分百》看过。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3691" title="together" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/together.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][5]

SHE现身，现场人声鼎沸呼声震天。四人合唱新专辑《SHERO》歌曲《两个人的荒岛》。身边有很多女生喜欢这首歌。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3692" title="IMG_0071" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/IMG_0071.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][6]

中景推一张。三人确实已经不再年轻了。毕竟都是快三十的人了，青春株式会社已然消解，女王的派头还能挺多久，就看歌迷买账与否了。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3693" title="IMG_0051" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/IMG_0051.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][7]

三人的服装造型很有意思。Selina的尤其有趣，背后还背着小翅膀。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3694" title="IMG_0074" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/IMG_0074.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][8]

三人排练的“最女王”的造型。不是孔雀舞，上面那手表示王冠，哈。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3695" title="IMG_0097" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/IMG_0097.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="375" />][9]

现场颇有“信射姐，得永生”的架势。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3696" title="_DSC0245" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/DSC0245.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="332" />][10]

还好我提前搞到张坐票，不然就得给挤成肉夹馍了。（照片 via 听雨）

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3697" title="_DSC0313" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/DSC0313.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="332" />][11]

又是从听雨上扒来的一张“女王”造型。Ella您的手这个褶子啊……

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3698" title="_DSC0403" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/DSC0403.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="332" />][12]

现场演唱了《两个人的荒岛》，《Shero》，《爱上你》，《中国话》，《Super Star》。《Super Star》虽然已经听她们唱了无数遍，现场依旧火爆，想必也只有这首歌最能代表三个人的巅峰时期。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" title="p_large_2bzY_26710001c4792d10" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/p_large_2bzY_26710001c4792d10.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="666" />][13]

三人学说内地网络流行语。Selina这句“不要迷恋姐，姐让你吐血”不知逗乐了多少人。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3700" title="_DSC0333" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/DSC0333.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="332" />][14]

似乎只有Ella最能对抗时间，除了新造型的发型前卫了点，其他变化不大。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3701" title="_DSC0389" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/DSC0389.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="332" />][15]

Hebe，哎。

[<img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone size-full wp-image-3702" title="p_large_1uAE_553800022b822d0d" src="http://www.fanbing.net/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/p_large_1uAE_553800022b822d0d.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="666" srcset="http://xdash.one/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/p_large_1uAE_553800022b822d0d.jpg 500w, http://xdash.one/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/p_large_1uAE_553800022b822d0d-300x400.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" />][16]

曾经最爱的Selina。
